IKIGAI - The secret of long life
There is a place on this planet called Okinawa, an island in Japan, where people live a longer life with rarely any disease.
Ikigai or 「生きがい」 literally means "something one lives for". It can be simply explained as "one's purpose in life".
A research was carried out to find the secret of longevity and vitality of the life of people of this island. The study revealed that these people had a PURPOSEFUL life. Each one had a purpose of getting out of the bed every morning. Each one had Ikigai.
IKIGAI means the reason to wake up in the morning. It improves your life by making it worth living each day because you have something to do for a reason to be alive. Therefore, there is no concept of retirement.
When people of Okinawa were asked what was their IKIGAI, few replies were as under:
-My IKIGAI is to watch my students improve
-I love taking care of my horses, so I wake up each morning.
-I love the feeling of my customer being happy
-When I see my children happy, I am happy.
These were the reasons that made people jump out of their bed each morning.
TO FIND OUT WHAT IS YOUR IKIGAI, ask four questions to yourself :
1. What I love?
2. What am I best at?
3. What does the world need from me?
4. What can I be paid for?
According to the Japanese, there's a motive behind your BIRTH. You have been sent because you have your role to play in something important. There is some purpose and your work is to find that purpose.
Q. How do we find the purpose ?
Let's simplify it more for better understanding.
a. Find out what you love to do, what is that skill you are devoted to ?
b. Next, look over it properly and see if you excel at the skill you claim to be your love.
c. Then find out if your skill/your love will result to be a good earning source for you.
d. At last, see if it helps you serve your nation with it in any way out.

इस ग्रह पर जापान में एक द्वीप ओकिनावा नामक एक जगह है, जहां लोग शायद ही कभी किसी बीमारी के साथ लंबा जीवन जीते हैं।
Ikigai या का शाब्दिक अर्थ है "कुछ जिसके लिए कोई रहता है"। इसे केवल "जीवन में किसी के उद्देश्य" के रूप में समझाया जा सकता है।
इस द्वीप के लोगों की लंबी उम्र और जीवन शक्ति का रहस्य खोजने के लिए एक शोध किया गया था। अध्ययन से पता चला कि इन लोगों का जीवन उद्देश्यपूर्ण था। हर एक का हर सुबह बिस्तर से उठने का एक उद्देश्य था। प्रत्येक के पास इकिगई था।
IKIGAI का अर्थ है सुबह उठने का कारण। यह आपके जीवन को हर दिन जीने लायक बनाकर बेहतर बनाता है क्योंकि आपको जीवित रहने के लिए कुछ करना होता है। इसलिए, सेवानिवृत्ति की कोई अवधारणा नहीं है।
